Powerful, light-weight, fast, free URL shortner

Built on Python, this is a simple yet powerful, light-weight, fast and free URL shortner.
It's currently under development, and is open-source on GitHub. Coded by ashishagarwal2023.

Why and What's coming?

This URL shortner service is based on a free 1-year NameCheap's student plan's custom domain. Neither it earn any profit for me, nor I got a credit card or bank account to recieve payments to, there are literally no ads, no data is shared, etc. To support me, just follow me on my GitHub.

I am working to add authentication (using email, you can login and signup). Logged in users can do any of these things: dashboard showing all your generated shorts, delete shorts or change their redirect URL, track the number of peoples who got redirected from your short, a simple API to do this all again, a python package for it, QR codes automated generation, custom-length and custom-slug links and more.

I will be working on these features, however it currently has: free generation of links, redirection, logging (server-side logging).
