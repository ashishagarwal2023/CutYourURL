Powerful, light-weight, fast, free URL shortner

Simple, light-weight and fast URL Shortener built in Python. This project aims to make a working bit.ly URL Shortener Clone and with more features or equal to as what bitly provides.

You can shorten your long URLs to a short one, and share it with your friends, family, etc. You can also track the clicks on your short links, and see the statistics of your short links.

You can also generate QR codes for your short links, and share it with your friends, family, etc. You can also see the statistics of your short links. You can also see the recent short links created on the server.

There's a lot more you can do and up there more is coming, It's currently under development!

Built by ashishagarwal, open-source on github.
